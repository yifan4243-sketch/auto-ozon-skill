# Russia seasonal and living-context guide

Use this guide as a small adjustment, not as a market-data replacement.

| Period | Typical context | Example eligible directions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jan–Feb | Cold weather, New Year aftermath, 23 February | home warmth, car winter accessories, practical gifts, indoor organization |
| Mar | 8 March gifting, transition weather | small gifts, beauty accessories, home organization |
| Apr–May | Dacha, renovation, spring cleaning, 1/9 May holidays | garden, storage, non-regulated home improvement, outdoor leisure |
| Jun–Aug | Summer travel, dacha, outdoor activity, back-to-school preparation | travel organizers, picnic/outdoor accessories, garden, school supplies late season |
| Sep | Back to school, cooler weather, home routines | stationery, organization, home comfort |
| Oct–Nov | Cold preparation, indoor living, seasonal sales | storage, home comfort, non-hazardous auto accessories |
| Dec | New Year gifting and preparation | giftable home, hobby, organization, seasonal décor when logistics permit |

Never infer that a category is viable solely from a seasonal match. Require a
positive score from the annual category snapshot first.
